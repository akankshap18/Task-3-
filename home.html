Services

Tasks and Lists

A task list is a project management tool that helps professionals keep track of their team's tasks. Task lists often include information such as the primary contact for the project, the professionals working on it and its deadline and status update.

Daily Planner

What's your plan for today?

Personal or work, plan and organize your tasks today. Adding your important must-do tasks to your schedule create an appointment for each task that blocks out the time you plan to spend working on the critical tasks.

Reminders

Set time based, location and recurring reminders

You can be reminded when individual reminders are due or when you arrive at or leave a location such as an address or your car. If you set both types of notifications for a reminder, you’re notified either at the time you specify or when you arrive at the location, whichever comes first.

For Office

Assign Tasks

Assign tasks and sub-tasks to promote business

Select the task to which you want to create the subtasks. Under Sub Tasks, you can Add a new sub-task and you can also select the Assignee, Due Date, Tags, and Priority.

Calendar View

Use calendar view to track due dates and deliverables

Track all your project activities using the Calendar module. Calendar is a simple and secure way to organize meetings, events, and manage tasks and issues.

Table View

Track your team’s progress visually with Table view

With the introduction of Task Table View, you and your team can view and manage work across various projects with ease.

Custom Views

Create a bird-eye view for all of your teams’ work

Custom views give team members the freedom to focus on the tasks that matter most. Whether you're an admin looking to oversee their team's progress.
